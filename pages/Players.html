Admirable people from our community

Doggie

Doggie is a highly skilled American player. considered to be one of the best players in the community, he has completed challenging top levels such as Cold Sweat, Acheron, Aerial Gleam, among others. He was recognized as the official verifier of Slaughterhouse (ex-top 1) after SpaceUK was caught hacking the level, which ended up in a big boost in popularity, making him stand out a lot more in the streaming part of the community.

Zoink

Zoink is an American player who is widely known for being the best GD player nowadays. Not only has he completed all the top 75 demons, but he has also verified the hardest levels in the list, such as Tidal Wave, Acheron, Avernus, etc. having low numbers in the total attempts per level completion. People most of the time find it difficult to believe that he is only 16 years old, and is still thought to be the most skilled player in the community.

Cursed

Cursed is a British player known for being really consistent with his completions. He has beaten really hard levels such as Abbys Of Darkness, SlaughterHouse, Kocmoc, Firework, and others. Furthermore, he has gained a big push in popularity by being the first victor of VSC, the hardest GD challenge to ever exist.

SrGuillester

SrGuillester is one of the most popular Spain GD players. He gained his own place in the community by making entertaining videos and challenges with different topics. Also known by his skills, "Guille" has completed levels such as Bloodbath, Cold Sweat, Killbot, etc. He is also famous for his creations, making really good layouts such as Abendregen, and really impressing levels such as Nhelv, considered to be his masterpiece.
