My Favourite Levels

-amazing pieces of art from this
incredible game-

-kocmoc-

A nine circles type of level, created by "CherryTeam".
Showing an UNBELIEVABLE space-themed decoration, alongside with a frenetic gameplay that manages to generate a really intense feeling all across the way, kocmoc is easily one of my most loved levels.

-overkill-

An almost wave exclusive level, created by "hawkyre & jinnans".
TOTAL madness, flashing red and white colors, exquisite usage of camera effects, as well as a really challenging and extreme gameplay. This is the reason why this unrealeased level gets its place in this list.

-new record-

Mouse-click destroyer, presented by "Temp & more".
Considered to be the most synchronized level of all times, new record shows a flashy, spammy gameplay, followed by a glorious technological decoration. A level that I really love, and hope to beat one day.

-tectonic tempo-

When speaking about bangers, tectonic tempo cannot fail to appear.
Created by "Jambees", this level has it all. Excellent usage of decoration, cool smooth transitions, along with a masterpiece of a song, this level easily stands out as one of my favourite creations.
